
The Jack Russell Terrier is one of the most recognizable and popular dog breeds in the world.
 Their charm and energy always attract attention. Curious, funny, friendly and playful - pets are always
 glad to new acquaintances and discoveries.

The artistry and bright personality of this breed is highly appreciated by the directors.
 Jack Russell Terriers became memorable movie characters, participating in the filming of numerous films,
 including such beloved and well-known to almost everyone films "The Mask" and "Difficult Child-2"
